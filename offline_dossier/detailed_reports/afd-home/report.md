AFD Home — Project-Driven Design & Architectural Solutions
AFD Home is a U.S.-based design, manufacturing, and project-management partner specializing in large-scale builder, developer, and hospitality projects. With more than three decades of experience, we collaborate closely with builders, architects, designers, and developers to deliver custom architectural elements, decorative installations, and statement pieces that elevate residential, commercial, and mixed-use environments. 
Our project portfolio spans entry gates, fencing, marble and stone elements, architectural detailing, large-scale sculptures, bespoke furnishings, and curated art programs, all engineered to integrate seamlessly into complex builds. From concept development through fabrication and installation coordination, AFD Home brings a solutions-oriented approach tailored to the demands of modern construction and design timelines. 
AFD Home has extensive experience working with restaurants, hospitality groups, and destination venues, producing durable, visually impactful pieces designed for high-traffic commercial environments. Our team understands builders requirements, scale, material performance, and the importance of cohesive design across multi-location rollouts. 
What sets AFD Home apart is our worldwide production and sourcing network paired with a highly experienced U.S.-based project management team. We manage global artisans, manufacturers, and logistics partners while serving as a single point of accountability for our clients. This structure allows us to execute projects efficiently, control quality, and meet aggressive schedules — even on large, multi-phase developments. 
For builders and developers seeking a trusted partner capable of managing custom scope, international production, and complex coordination, AFD Home delivers proven expertise, dependable execution, and distinctive design at scale.
