## IBS Description
The American Institute of Building Design (AIBD) is the national professional association for residential building designers, established in 1950 to advance and protect the building design profession. 
Who AIBD Represents
AIBD represents design professionals who specialize in single-family and light commercial residential projects, including custom homes, remodeling, and community-scale design. Members include Certified Professional Building Designers, architects, engineers, and related design professionals committed to high standards of practice and ethics. 
Mission and Focus
AIBD’s mission is to protect and enhance its members’ ability to practice building design while promoting excellence, innovation, and consumer choice in residential design. The organization develops and upholds nationwide design standards and a code of ethics that support quality, safety, and professionalism in the built environment. 
Education and Certification
AIBD provides robust continuing education to keep members current with evolving technologies, materials, sustainability practices, and building codes. Through its related certification programs, it recognizes professionals who demonstrate verified knowledge, experience, and a strong commitment to ongoing professional development. 
Value to Builders and Consumers
Builders and developers partnering with AIBD members gain access to designers who understand constructability, cost-efficiency, and marketability, helping projects move smoothly from concept to completion. For homeowners and buyers, the AIBD name signals a trusted design professional dedicated to functional, beautiful, and code-compliant residential environments.
