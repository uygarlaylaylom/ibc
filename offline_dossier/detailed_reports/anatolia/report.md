Anatolia is a Canadian-based global company specializing in the design, manufacturing, and distribution of sintered slabs, ceramic and porcelain tiles, natural stone products, mosaics, surface protectors and related accessories to some of the largest distributors and national DIY chains throughout the globe.

This family-owned and operated enterprise provides its customers access to an extensive stable inventory, a superior product line as well as a customer centric approach.
