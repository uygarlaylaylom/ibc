## IBS Description
Thank you for taking the time to visit our booth and learn about our kitchen products  and wardrobe system . Our company started in Taiwan in 1995. and now we have four factories in China with 30 years of experience. We’ve built a solid reputation in this industry. Our main markets are in North America and EU. Such as The Home Depot, Lowe’s, Costco, B&Q, Brico Depot. 
Our company offers two main product lines: 
The first is a metal wardrobe system, a fully DIY modular solution that is more environmentally friendly and cost-efficient compared to traditional wooden furniture. It is suitable for a wide range of spaces, including under-stair areas, garages, bathrooms, and office rooms. Our wardrobe systems combine flexibility, modern design, and affordability. 
The second product line consists of kitchen metal baskets. An essential helper for kitchen organization, it greatly enhances the storage capacity for kitchen items and makes kitchen storage more convenient and efficient.
