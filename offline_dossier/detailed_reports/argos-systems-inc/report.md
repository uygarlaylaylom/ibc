## IBS Description
Vertex BD is a professional BIM software ideal for wood and cold-formed steel framers, component manufacturers, modular home builders, and production homes builders.

Production home builders leverage its industry leading house options management system to automate the production of lot specific and master drawing sets, material reports, and marketing materials. 
Vertex BD Building Design software executes many of the repetitive, time-consuming tasks that slow down your work.
