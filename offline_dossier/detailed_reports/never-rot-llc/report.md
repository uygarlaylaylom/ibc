## IBS Description
Never Rot™ – Permanent Protection for Garage Door Jambs
Never Rot™ is a durable garage door jamb protection system designed to eliminate one of the most common failure points in residential construction: wood rot at ground level. Engineered for both new construction and retrofit applications, Never Rot installs seamlessly to protect garage door frames from moisture damage—permanently. 
Installed flush with the ground, Never Rot creates a tight, continuous seal with no gaps, preventing water intrusion while also blocking pests, reptiles, and rodents from entering the garage. The result is a cleaner finish, longer-lasting materials, and fewer callbacks for builders and contractors. 
Whether incorporated during framing or added to existing homes, Never Rot delivers a simple, effective solution that extends the life of garage door jambs and improves overall home durability.
