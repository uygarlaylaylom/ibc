## IBS Description
For over 55 years, Bestbath® has helped builders, remodelers, and multifamily teams deliver durable, high-quality bathing solutions, without adding complexity to the build. 
From single-piece, pre-accessorized showers and tubs that install direct-to-stud, to multi-piece wall and pan systems designed for efficient remodels, Bestbath® products are built to simplify installation, reduce rework, and hold up in real-world use. Our reinforced fiberglass composite units feature a solid wood core for strength, a durable gelcoat or HPL surface, and are backed by a 30-year warranty. 
Manufactured entirely in the USA at our Idaho and Tennessee facilities, Bestbath® offers dependable lead times—shipping many standard units in as little as one week and full truckloads in under three—so your projects stay on schedule and crews stay productive.
