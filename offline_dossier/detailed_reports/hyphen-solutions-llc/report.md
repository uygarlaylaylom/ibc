## IBS Description
Centered around Home Builders, Suppliers, Manufacturers and Trades, Hyphen provides the only full-cycle software suite designed to support your businesses before, during and beyond the build.   
The top North American Home Builders trust Hyphen Solutions, the makers of BuildPro, SupplyPro Hyphen Wallet, and more, as their reliable resource in the residential construction industry. Hyphen’s software-as-a-service delivers greater operational control, secure payment solutions and increased productivity to every size Builder, Supplier, Manufacturer, and Trade. In 2024, the Hyphen Network of 615+ Builders and 18,000+ residential Suppliers built 1 in 3 new homes using Hyphen’s Home Builder and Supply Chain solutions, making Hyphen the leading cloud-based residential construction management software provider.  
We are excited to feature Hyphen Wallet, BuilderGM, SupplyPro GM, Hyphen HomeSight, Hyphen CRM, and BRIX, and showcase our full suite of solutions  and services including our flagship products BuildPro and SupplyPro.   

Hyphen Wallet, our ePayments software, secures and accelerates payments from Builders to their Suppliers with integrated digital payments. With this simple solution, payment safety is a worry of the past. Builders can also eliminate time-consuming check runs, payment delays and uncertainty while gaining critical visibility and control of the payment process. A simple portal provides Suppliers unparalleled control of their cash flow with instant visibility into Builder payment and the ability to select how and when to get paid for completed work. Learn more about the value-added features that make Hyphen Wallet stand out from competitors by visiting info.hyphensolutions.com.  


BuilderGM, part of our Construction Management solution set, is a powerful all-in-one solution designed specifically for small to mid-size custom, local and regional Home Builders, as well as remodelers, providing comprehensive management tools to streamline operations, enhance collaboration, and drive efficiency. BuilderGM provides solutions for estimating, scheduling, bidding, vendor management, client billing, document management, accounting integration through QuickBooks and more. As an all-in-one software solution to simplify Home Builders’ workflow from concept to keys as a cloud-based software, BuilderGM can be used anytime, anywhere. With an intuitive work structure that flows from one activity to the next, in sequence, and pushes your job information through the pipeline with the click of a button. 


SupplyPro GM, part of our Orders and Field Management and Supply Chain Management solutions sets, optimizes productivity, reduces costs, and enables real-time information exchange on a unified cloud platform. Whether you're a new user or experienced SupplyPro user, activating SupplyPro GM is a natural progression. This intuitive platform streamlines operations by seamlessly connecting field and office. With the mobile app, Suppliers efficiently manage their business, empowering employees to perform anywhere, anytime, on any device. Take charge of your business and enhance operations with instant updates delivered straight to your mobile device. 


Hyphen HomeSight, part of our Marketing and Sales and Design solution sets, is an advanced, interactive design solution for Home Builders to offer a hands-on experience of their communities and homes anytime and anyplace. Giving homebuyers the power to design a home with every option and instant life-like outcomes builds confidence, increases the likelihood of upgrades and delivers website leads with detailed insights. Builders enjoy an increased lead-to-sales conversion rate, accelerated sales cycles and increased efficiency with design center time decreased by 67% -- all with integrations into your website, design center and back-office systems. 


Hyphen CRM, part of our Marketing and Sales solution sets, is a cloud-based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform built by Home Builders for Home Builders to optimize customer acquisition, coordinate and improve marketing efforts, and drive sales. Hyphen CRM brings sales and marketing data into an easy-to-use platform with two funnels for homebuyers and real estate agents and includes tools for newsletter publishing, email campaigns, campaign results and more. Home Builders invest significant budget and resources into sales and marketing, and with Hyphen CRM you can efficiently and effectively nurture leads throughout the home purchase journey. 


Hyphen BRIX, part of our Accounting and Operations solutions sets, is a fully integrated Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solution with accounting for North American Home Builders. Used to issue 543,000+ cloud-based platform provides solutions for accounting, purchasing, estimating, sales, scheduling, automated tax calculation and more. Connecting field staff with the back-office allows Home Builders to streamline communications, automate routine tasks, speed up production and minimize mistakes.
