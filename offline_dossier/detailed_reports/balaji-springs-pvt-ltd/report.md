## IBS Description
Balaji Springs is India’s leading manufacturer of custom springs since 1967, proudly serving the automotive, agriculture, garage door, and roll-up door sectors alongside building and construction industries . 
We specialize in manufacturing high-performance springs and building hardware for a wide range of applications, including door closures, canopies, garage and roll-up doors, umbrella tables, architectural hardware, and various building requirements. Our products are engineered for durability, safety, and consistent performance in demanding environments. 
With a weekly production capacity of 1.43 million springs, our state-of-the-art 108,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced machinery from Germany, Italy, and Taiwan and supported by 267 skilled professionals. We produce springs with inner diameters ranging from 1.75” to 6”, across multiple lengths and wire sizes, using premium materials such as A228 and A229 for superior strength and longevity. 
Balaji Springs is a trusted partner to leading global OEMs including CNH Industrial, Doosan Bobcat, Hendrickson, BPW, SAF Holland, Meritor, John Deere Ancillary, Seco Komos, and many others. 
What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality, reliability, and value. By sourcing premium raw materials from reputed mills and maintaining competitive pricing, we consistently exceed industry standards and build long-term partnerships worldwide.
