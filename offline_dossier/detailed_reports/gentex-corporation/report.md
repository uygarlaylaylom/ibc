## IBS Description
Gentex Corporation, founded in 1974 and headquartered in Zeeland, Michigan, is a leading global technology and manufacturing company specializing in digital vision systems, connected-vehicle technology, dimmable glass, fire protection solutions, medical devices, and consumer electronics. The company is best known for pioneering electrochromic, automatic-dimming rearview mirrors for the automotive industry. 
Gentex is vertically integrated, with capabilities spanning microelectronics, imaging and sensing technology, glass processing, chemical coatings, automated assembly, and software design — enabling rapid innovation and high-volume OEM production. Gentex leverages these core competencies, along with strategic partnerships, acquisitions, and ongoing research to create market-leading positions in a variety of verticals. With more than 6,000 employees across its Zeeland and West Michigan facilities, Gentex continues to be a major regional employer while advancing its global presence through new technologies, markets, and innovation.
