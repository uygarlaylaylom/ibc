## IBS Description
NA Structures offers solutions related to framing custom homes. 
Our solutions relate to helping builders to avoid typical issues related to framing complex custom homes …including the running of HVAC system, MEP’s, placement of windows and doors, recessed lighting etc.  Our technical team takes your 2D plans and creates a 3d model and finds any possible interference and presents a solution to the project team before the build. 
When all solutions are agreed upon and everything is signed off on by the project team, we custom fabricate, off-site in our plant, all the framing components…from sill plates to roof sheathing along with panelized walls, floor systems, trusses and rafters. The result is less labor and time spent on site framing the project in, plus you will have a very precise structure without typical framing issues that have to be worked out on site. 
Our system helps to ensure that you control your project, the project doesn’t control you. 
NA Structures works in most every state and has also worked on custom residential projects abroad.
