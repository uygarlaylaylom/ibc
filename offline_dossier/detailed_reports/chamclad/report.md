## IBS Description
ChamClad® was founded with a clear purpose: to deliver premium, Canadian-made Architectural Building Products crafted from 100% engineered recycled window-grade PVC. Our commitment to sustainability empowers architects and developers with LEED-contributing products—ranging from cladding and soffit to columns, beams, privacy walls, and interior panels. 
Innovation drives everything we do. In 2023, we introduced our 2-piece Column Wrap and Faux Beam, followed by the revolutionary Quick Click Series in 2024. This series includes the award-winning Quick Click 6" Shadowline Soffit, recognized as Best Outdoor Product at IBS, along with our versatile Quick Click Column and Beam system designed for ultimate customization.
