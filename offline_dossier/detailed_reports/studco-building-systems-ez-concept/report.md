## IBS Description
Studco® designs, develops, and manufactures quality steel framing systems, architectural finishing sections and sound isolation clips for the building and construction industry. 
Since opening our first factory in 1986 as a steel stud roll-former in Melbourne, Australia, Studco has developed into a global manufacturer of premium building systems for commercial and residential construction. Today Studco is located in the USA, Canada, Australia and New Zealand, including a local manufacturing plant in Rochester, NY; our warehouses are fully stocked and ready to supply your project. 
The Studco team is committed to being more than just another building product supplier. Our team works with customers to ensure each project runs smoothly, stands out and is a great success. Choose Studco Building Systems® for your next project and experience the proactive approach to delivering a cost-effective and innovative steel solution. 
Manufactured by Studco Building Systems®, EZ Concept® are the innovators of the world renowned EzyJamb® door jamb systems, offering an expanding range of trim-free architectural finishing products to create modern interiors for residential, industrial and commercial spaces. 
With our innovative line of products, we are here to help architects, interior designers, builders and developers achieve contemporary flush finished designs. 
Resilmount® sound isolation clips and hangers are manufactured by Studco Building Systems® and cover a wide range of effective solutions for noise isolation for the wall and ceiling industry.
