## IBS Description
Founded in 1949, Nu-Wool was created with a simple vision: create superior cellulose insulation and lead the insulation industry toward sustainability and excellence. For over 75 years, Nu-Wool has manufactured premium cellulose insulation products and has provided unmatched service and expertise, while developing true partnerships with our installers and distributors. Throughout our history, Nu-Wool has been a family-owned company, and that intentional and genuine family-feel is an integral part of who we are. Nu-Wool's success has largely been rooted in our intentionality and exceptional customer service offered alongside the premium, sustainable insulation products we make. 
Over the years, Nu-Wool has continually innovated and adapted, diversifying our products to go beyond the traditional cellulose insulation we started with. Nu-Wool's product line includes: Nu-Wool Premium Cellulose Insulation, ECOCELL Batts, ECOCELL Blankets, INSULDRI Foundation Insulation & Drainage Board, Nu-Cell Spray Applied Cellulose, Nu-Wool WALLSEAL Fire & Sound Cellulose Insulation, and Nu-Seal Polyurethane Spray Foam.  
As the oldest cellulose insulation manufacturer in the world, Nu-Wool takes pride in being a leader of the cellulose insulation industry. Our long-standing commitment to excellence--both in service and products--has allowed us to thrive and grow over our many years of operations!
