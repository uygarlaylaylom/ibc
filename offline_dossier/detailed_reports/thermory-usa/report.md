## IBS Description
Thermory, a global leader in manufacturing thermally modified wood and sauna materials, has specialized in thermal modification for more than twenty-five years, combining a scientific approach with functional design to create beautiful, long-lasting solutions for those who appreciate natural living. Thermal modification is a chemical-free process that uses heat and steam to create beautiful, real wood products with unequaled strength and durability. Thermory sources all of its wood using responsible forestry and provides naturally renewable building materials that do not generate hazardous waste.
