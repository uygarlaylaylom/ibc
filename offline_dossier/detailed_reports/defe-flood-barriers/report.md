## IBS Description
DEFE Flood Barriers is an Irish company based in Wicklow, dedicated to providing heavy-duty, compact, and durable flood protection solutions since 2018. Our barriers are specifically engineered to prevent water and flood damage for a wide range of premises, including residential doors and windows, underground entrances, car parks, schools, and public service amenities. 
We specialize in customized, made-to-measure flood barriers, tailored to the unique needs of homes, businesses, and communities in flood-risk areas. Our products are rigorously tested to comply with the PAS 1188 standard, ensuring safety, durability, and affordability for our clients.
