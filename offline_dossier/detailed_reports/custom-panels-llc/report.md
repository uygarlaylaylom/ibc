## IBS Description
Custom Panels has a long history of making oversized  and cut-to- size  panels made from stable Western Douglas Fir plywood.    We also have the ability to laminate paper overlays to mask the grain for painting, along with cold pressing thicker laminates.      Custom edge and face grooving are also in our bag of tricks!    For example, we can  T&G  for edges of a plywood panels, while also custom grooving  (think T-111 plywood siding)   
  
Some of the products we make: 
Stair Stringers   
Exterior Trim 
Pontoon Boat Decks 
Bus and Trailer Decks and Sides 
Arena Panels 
Oversized  1 pc  Wall panels  
Mezzanine Decks  
  
Please discuss with us your possible proprietary needs.    Dreams can come true! 
See you at the show!
