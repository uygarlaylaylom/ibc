## IBS Description
MUCHTEK is an Argentine company with a strong track record and broad recognition in the market. Its core essence lies in constant investment in innovation and cutting-edge technology, offering a wide range of high-quality and highly efficient PVC profile systems for windows, doors, and architectural products. 
With over 20 years of experience in the construction industry, MUCHTEK has established itself as the leading brand in the development of high-performance PVC profile systems, meeting the demands of the Argentine and Latin American markets. Its exponential growth is reflected in its presence in more than 15 countries across the region, with subsidiaries in Brazil, Chile, and Mexico, and it is currently expanding its presence in the United States. 
In recent years, the company has expanded its business units. In addition to its Profiles line, MUCHTEK introduced IN&OUT Design, featuring architectural PVC products such as tubular profiles, wall panels, decks, and cladding systems. It also launched MUCHTEK Recycling, a circular economy initiative that allows the recycling and reuse of three to four tons of PVC profiles per day, reintegrating them into the market as auxiliary profiles, battens, and baseboards. Through this initiative, MUCHTEK has successfully incorporated sustainability and circular economy principles into its business strategy. 
MUCHTEK is the leading manufacturer and marketer of PVC profiles for windows and architectural products in Latin America. 
With over 270 direct employees and a commercial presence in 16 countries, MUCHTEK operates an integrated industrial complex in Pablo Podestá, Buenos Aires, Argentina. The facility includes a 7,000 m² extrusion plant with 9 extrusion lines, a 2,000 m² dedicated lamination plant equipped with 5 foil-wrapping lines, and a 3,000 m² logistics and distribution center. 
The company reaches a production capacity of 12,000 tons of PVC profiles per year, supporting the installation of over 360,000 windows annually. This scale has positioned MUCHTEK as a regional benchmark for high-performance PVC solutions in contemporary architecture. 
Driven by innovation, energy efficiency, and design excellence, MUCHTEK continues to expand its international footprint, bringing its industrial expertise to the most demanding global markets.
