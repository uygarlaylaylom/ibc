## IBS Description
As a global leader in accessibility, Savaria is all about empowering better mobility for life. At IBS 2026, we’re putting a spotlight on luxury with the Savaria Vuelift panoramic glass home elevator. Come see our working demo unit and learn more about the aesthetic impact and extreme floorplan flexibility that makes this unique elevator the centerpiece of home design. 
Also on display? Our traditional home elevators and our innovative and beautiful new touchscreen car operating panels (COPs). With interfaces designed to put an elegant touch on residential elevator cab aesthetics, these high-definition digital panels modernize the user experience. 
And be sure to visit our sister company, Garaventa Lift, in Booth W6147 to view the beautiful Savaria Luma home lift demo unit!  
With one of the largest mobility and accessibility portfolios on the market, Savaria designs and manufactures a range of wheelchair lifts, elevators and stairlifts, for both commercial and residential applications, as well as patient care products. Our acquisition of Matot in 2024 saw the addition of world-class dumbwaiters and material lifts to our product portfolio. 
Our in-house engineering and expertise is backed by a network of over 600 dealers across North America—with additional representation across Europe, Australia and Asia—who provide both local code knowledge and best-in-class installation and service for your projects.
