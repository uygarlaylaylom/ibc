## IBS Description
Energy Saving Products manufactures high-quality air handlers and duct distribution systems engineered for high-efficiency heating and cooling in condos, homes, and commercial buildings. With more than 40 years in business, the company provides comprehensive duct design services and system selection support to help ensure optimal performance in every application. 
Hi-Velocity Systems operate on higher static pressures than conventional ductwork, powered by specially designed fan motors. The product line includes specialized air handlers with optional heating and cooling coils, allowing flexible integration with tankless water heaters, hot water tanks, air-to-water heat pumps, geothermal systems, boilers, and more. The company also offers modulating electric heater kits, zoning systems, and designer vent plates that match virtually any décor. Compatible with both DX and hydronic heat pumps, the systems are known for simple installation and setup, making them a preferred choice for installers seeking combination heating/hot-water solutions or dual-fuel configurations with hydronic backup to a heat pump. For multi-unit projects, their builder-series models deliver cost savings with unpainted galvanized cabinets while maintaining the company’s established build quality. In addition, they supply indoor air quality solutions designed to eliminate mold and mildew.
