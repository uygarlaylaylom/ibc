## IBS Description
In business since 1955, Hoover Treated Wood Products, Inc. ("Hoover") manufactures a comprehensive portfolio of pressure-impregnated, kiln-dried  wood products, such as lumber, plywood, glulam, and LVL products at 10 of their treating facilities throughout the United States. Their primary focus is on fire-retardant-treated wood.  
Hoover manufactures Pyroguard and ExteriorFireX fire-retardant-treated wood for interior-protected and weather-exposed applications, respectively. ExteriorFireX meets the provisions of International Wildland Urban Interface Code (IWUIC). PyroGuard and ExteriorFireX lumber and plywood offer the economy, workability, and versatility of wood as well as the fire-safety and insurance advantages of noncombustible materials.
