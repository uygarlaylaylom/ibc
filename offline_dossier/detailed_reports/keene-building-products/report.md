## IBS Description
Keene Building Products specializes in innovative solutions for building envelope moisture management, floor and ceiling soundproofing, and waterproofing/drainage for foundations, balconies and green roof. As a pioneer in entangled net products, Keene Building Products enhances drainage, ventilation, and isolation in walls, masonry, foundations, and plazas for both residential and commercial construction. 
Our product lineup includes: Driwall™ Rainscreens and Easy Fur® furring strips ensure superior moisture management, drainage, and ventilation, promoting long-term durability and compliance; Quiet Qurl® sound control mats and GSL® self-leveling underlayments, helping meet IIC and STC sound ratings affordably; and Balcony waterproofing systems combine sound control and moisture protection for seamless multifamily solutions. 
With American-made products, expert support, and a nationwide distribution network, Keene Building Products accelerates construction timelines and lowers costs. From sound control to waterproofing, Keene delivers tailored solutions for successful single and multi-family developments.
