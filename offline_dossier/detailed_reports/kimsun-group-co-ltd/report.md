The company particularly highlights its "new coordinated color and finish whole-home door customization", allowing consumers to match door products to their floor color, achieving true harmony in living spaces. 
Kimsun's design team keeps pace with international trends, continuously introducing "youthful, fashionable, and minimalist" design series to meet the needs of modern consumers seeking personalized home decor. These designs focus not only on visual appeal but also carefully consider space utilization and functionality, making each door and window a focal point of home artistry. 
Technological R&D, Industry Leadership 
Kimsun maintains a strategy of "technology-driven development". It has established the "Kimsun Door Design Center" in Italy and the "Kimsun Door Engineering and Technology R&D Center" in Germany. These international R&D institutions provide the company with access to world-leading design concepts and process technologies.

This innovation not only ensures stable product quality and environmental performance from the source but also significantly improves the delivery efficiency of custom products.
