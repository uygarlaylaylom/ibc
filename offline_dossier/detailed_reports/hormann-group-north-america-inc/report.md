As a family business for nearly a century, more than 20 million doors have been produced and installed since 1935. Here in North America, Hörmann operates six modern manufacturing facilities, 23 sectional door distribution centers, and we are enthusiastic about delivering the most complete range of quality, up-to-date door, and dock system solutions available. Our innovations and options make it possible to accommodate renovation and new construction specifications where people live and work - no matter what. 
Whether it is a renovation or new construction project, our high-performance and sectional doors and loading dock systems are designed to integrate seamlessly within many markets including manufacturing, retail automotive, parking, and self-storage.  For residential use, we manufacture an exciting variety of garage door models, with options and styles that satisfy all tastes, from economical builder-grade to highly customized luxury designs.

In addition, we have an architectural support staff ready to assist with design and application questions so you can specify our products with confidence. 
Our team of sales, design, and dealer professionals are ready to help you determine the best solution for your operational goals and budget, and we have trained installers and service experts positioned across North America for convenient access to local assistance.
