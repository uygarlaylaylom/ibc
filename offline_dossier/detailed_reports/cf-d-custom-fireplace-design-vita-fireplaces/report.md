## IBS Description
CF + D custom fireplace design has mastered the skills and technology required to design and fabricate highly innovative, one-of-a-kind fireplaces often in dimensions and finishes that have been created for the first time. With a team of highly skilled craftsmen proficient in the art of shaping, fabricating and welding steel materials CF + D is committed to breaking the paradigms and pushing the boundaries of fireplace design possibilities with Quality Creativity and Innovation  
 Now as a full design/build manufacturing company, CF + D has introduced their own production line of artistic contemporary designs with our VITA | Fireplaces collection. North Americas first lineup of  Suspended and Suspended Hood with Base models tested and certified in-house for all fuel types; Gas (NG or LP), Vapor-Fire (Water fueled/Electric) and Wood Burning. 
 Also included in our line up of fire features is our collection of Linear Built-In models incorporated the Vapor-Fire burner technology system using water/electric as a fuel source. From Single-Sided to See-Thru and now introducing our Multi-View Grandview collection, Panorama, Pier, Right Side and Left Side open. 
Visit our web site www.customfireplacedesign.com or meet us at the Show Booth#S13023
