## IBS Description
AluGarden is a registered brand created by Alcas Metal for its pergola and shading systems product group. 
AluGarden has developed aluminum-made shading, deck, garden fence, cotta, sunbreaker, and guillotine window systems for use in gardens and terraces, in response to demands and trends. These products have been designed with an emphasis on safety, comfort, and functionality, creating a large ecosystem that decoratively complements each other. 
The most important feature of our products is that they can be assembled by the consumer with their family members without the need for a professional team or supervisor. Our products are manufactured with wood patterns and colors that are closest to natural wood.
