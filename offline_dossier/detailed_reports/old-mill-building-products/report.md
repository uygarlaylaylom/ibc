## IBS Description
Old Mill Building Products designs real-material building systems that make construction faster, simpler, and more reliable. Founded in 2007 as Old Mill Brick, the company expanded beyond thin brick and rebranded in 2023 to reflect a broader portfolio of integrated building solutions. In 2024, Old Mill opened a new manufacturing facility in American Fork, Utah to support national growth and tighter quality control. 
Old Mill operates through three focused brands: 


Old Mill BrickReal clay thin brick and the patented Brickwebb® system, which allows installers to place up to twelve bricks at once for faster, more consistent results. 


Old Mill SystemsHigh-performance wall assemblies including the Panel+ insulated wall system, air and water barriers, adhesives, and fasteners designed to simplify installation and meet modern energy and fire code requirements. 


Old Mill AccentsStone ledgers and designer tile products for interior and exterior applications that add texture and architectural character. 


Across residential, commercial, and mixed-use projects, Old Mill products help reduce labor time and total project costs while delivering the look and durability of real brick and stone. The company serves architects, builders, contractors, retailers, and homeowners nationwide.
