## IBS Description
Founded in 2002, Pneumatic Vacuum Elevators, LLC (PVE) are the original inventors and pioneers of the worlds first air-driven home elevator, the Vacuum Elevator. 
Powered by the most abundant resource in the world, AIR, Vacuum Elevators are innovative, space-saving home lift solutions that are economical alternative to stairs. 
Using Pneumatic Technology® to generate lift, these eco-friendly home elevators smoothly move passengers between floors through air-suction and gravity. 
Requiring much less space, energy and maintenance than tradition home elevators, these self-supporting home lifts simply rest on the existing ground floor of the home, are plugged into a 220 volt single phase power supply and can be installed in as little as 2 - 3 days. 
With four sizes to choose from, ranging from a single passenger to a three passenger wheelchair friendly model, Vacuum Elevators are custom built to the floor-to-floor heights of each home with PVE's round elevators able to travel up to 5 stops or 50 feet in total rise. 
As "The Only Square Powered By Air", The Cube can travel up to 3 stops or 35 feet in rise. 
Combining a compact footprint with an elegant design, these panoramic elevators offer 360° visibility while traveling, optimizing space within the home while enhancing the interior design of any room. 
Headquartered in Miami, Florida, USA, PVE has over 28,000 successful installations worldwide in over 100 countries. With subsidiaries in Europe & India, PVE utilizes materials and components of the highest quality to produce fully certified, safe and reliable home elevators for use worldwide. 
Partnered with authorized distributors across the globe, PVE ensures all end users the best quality installation and service for their home elevators.
