## IBS Description
TOP HARDWARES is a company focusing on manufacturing and exporting door hinge, locks and other hardware accessories. TOP enjoys a good reputation among its customers and partners globally including many well-known companies, because it has been dedicated to providing high quality hardware with affordable prices, and also because it has products covering a wide spectrum of sizes and different materials such as iron, stainless steel, brass, aluminum, zinc alloy etc.
 
Started hinges production and business since 1980s. The first manufacturing site was in Shenzhen, Guangdong. With the help of abundant local resources, the site mainly produced stainless steel hinges and the products were exported to North and South America.
 
In 1998, the manufacturing plant was moved to Pinghu, in Zhejiang province and started to have its own electroplating plant, producing commercial, residential hinges, and spring hinges. The factory can then serve various needs from customers including duty hinges, T hinges, welding hinges, invisible & 3D hinges, functional hinges, as well as customized hinges.
 
From 2005, TOP start to attend the domestic and overseas famous hardwares fairs to introduce our products, TOP devote to providing better hardware and making service for more clients.
 
TOP means Excellent, and its Chinese pronunciation has meaning of exploration and practical. These meanings nicely manifested TOP' s commitment to its customers and its product quality.
  
In 2018, the brand TOBALIN was registered. In the same year, TOP invested locks and handles factory in Wenzhou. Up till now, Locks series have included tubular locks grade 3, deadbolts, economic knob/levers, grip handles, mortise locks, rim locks, aluminum & zinc alloy handles, stainless steel handles, cylinders that satisfy clients' various hardware demand.
 
With more than 30 years of efforts and persistence, TOP now has hundreds of employees with a complete system of manufacturing, management, sales and quality control. TOP partners with companies in more than 40 different countries including door manufacturers in America, supermarkets in Europe, construction companies in Japan, wholesalers in middle east and etc.
 
Under the TOP's professional exporting teams and leadership, TOP continues providing the best hinge, locks and hardware solutions to its customers. TOP aims at offering top quality products with reasonable prices as well as the satisfaction of its customers.
