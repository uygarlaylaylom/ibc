## IBS Description
About FEI Electric
At FEI Electric, we combine over 36 years of expertise with a commitment to quality, innovation, and service. As a factory‐affiliate supplier, we specialize in delivering premium electrical and lighting solutions designed to meet the evolving needs of residential, multifamily, commercial, and industrial customers. 
What We Do


Outlet Boxes — We offer both metallic and non‐metallic boxes, including PVC and PC non‐metallic types. Our product lines include single‑gang, double‑gang, three‑ and four‑gang boxes, old‑work and new‑work styles, fan boxes, ceiling boxes, and more.  


Fiberglass Boxes — For specialized applications, we also supply a variety of fiberglass outlet boxes in multiple gang sizes and shapes (including round) to meet demanding insulation, exposure, or durability requirements.  


Lighting Fixtures — Our lighting offerings span several categories: ceiling fans, ceiling‑mounted fixtures, outdoor lights, pendants, track/spot lighting, vanity lights, and wall lamps.  


Electrical Devices & Wiring — We produce wires suited for conduit and cable‑tray use, rated for commercial or industrial applications. Our USB receptacles and other devices include modern safety and performance features like heat detection and multi‑port charging.  


HVAC & Duct Products — We support the heating, ventilation, and air conditioning sector with a broad catalog: insulated flexible ducts, foil flexible ducts, PET insulated ducts, non‑woven and nylon fire‑retardant flexible ducts, semi‑rigid and stainless steel ducts, and various sound or joint silencers.
