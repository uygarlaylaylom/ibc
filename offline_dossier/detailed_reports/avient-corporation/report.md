## IBS Description
Avient: Next-Generation Composite Solutions for Offsite Construction 
Avient delivers innovative composite materials that solve real construction challenges – reducing labor costs, accelerating project timelines, and improving durability. 
Why Choose Composites? 
Our engineered composite panels and components outperform conventional materials where it matters most: 

Resistant to moisture, corrosion, rot, and UV damage
Lightweight, large-format panels engineered for strength and consolidated installation
Minimal maintenance – Lower lifetime costs for your clients

Hammerhead™ FR Flame Retardant Composite Sandwich Panels are the first in the industry to meet ASTM E84 Class A standards for flame spread and smoke density. Select configurations also meet NFPA 286 corner room burn requirements – all with integrated flame retardant technology. 
Military-grade fiber-reinforced polymer panels deliver superior bullet resistance for safe rooms and panic rooms – at a fraction of the weight of steel or concrete. The result? Safer handling and faster installation. 
Visit us at Booth W5783 to experience our Composites Innovation Showcase. See composite materials in action through real-world applications including interior walls, exterior sheathing, composite decking and more.
